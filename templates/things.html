Things to do

There are so many places in Nashik to visit. Here are some our recommendations that we have experienced.

Vaitarna River

Held by the Adventure Camp Zone on the Vaitarna River, the camp has the best facilities to provide a wonderful experience.

How to reach: The distance between Nashik and Vaitarna river is approximately 140 kilometers and can be covered by both private and public in 2-3 hours.

  • Time required: 2.5 hours
  • Address: Vaitarna River, Nashik, Maharashtra
  • Timings: 10:00 AM and 5:30 PM
Rating: 4.2 ★ | Visit on Google Map

Sula Vineyards

Embark on a memorable wine tour with your family or friends and choose to go on a guided tour.

How to reach: The best way to reach the Sula Vineyards from major cities like Pune or Mumbai is by the Mumbai-Agra National Highway (NH3). You can cover the distance either by train, bus or car.

  • Time required: 2 hours
  • Address: Gate 36/2, Govardhan Village, Off Gangapur-Savargaon Road, Nashik
  • Timings: 12:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Rating: 4.3 ★ | Visit on Google Map

Zonkers Adventure Park

Nestled near the scenic backwaters of the Gangapur Dam, this park is an ideal adventure destination and houses facilities for an entire day of fun.

How to reach: If you are traveling from Nashik, take the Gangapur Rd and Gangapur-Savargaon Rd for a 40-minute ride that will take you to Gangapur dam where the adventure part is situated.

  • Time required: 1 hour
  • Address: Opposite Beyond Resort By Sula, Gangapur-Savargaon Road, Gangavhare, Nashik
  • Timings: 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Rating: 4.1 ★ | Visit on Google Map

Coin Museum

Spanning across an area of 505 acres of land, the Coin Museum is located on the campus of the Indian Institute of Research in Numismatic Studies.

How to reach: Approximately 20 kilometers from the central square in Nashik. Public transport such as bus and train are available from most stations in the city.

  • Time required: 1 hour
  • Address: Opposite Grape County Resort Nashik
  • Timings: 9:30 AM and 5:30 PM
Rating: 4.3 ★ | Visit on Google Map

Trimbakeshwar Temple

Located on the banks of River Godavari at the foothills of Brahmagiri Mountains, the Trimbakeshwar Temple is one among the 12 Jyotirlinga temples of Lord Shiva

How to reach: The distance between Nashik and Trimbakeshwar temple is 28 kilometers and can make a good road trip as many travelers drive themselves or hire a cab to the site.

  • Address: Trimbak, Maharashtra 422212
  • Timings: 5:30 AM - 9:00 PM
Rating: 5 ★ | Visit on Google Map
